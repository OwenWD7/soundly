Soundly - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Soundly ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account, we access the following information via TikTok's official API:

  • Basic profile information (username, profile picture)
  • Your liked videos and associated sound metadata (title, author, cover art)

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or any sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Display your personal sound library within the app
  • Enable playlist creation and audio playback features
  • Improve the app experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Data Storage

Your library data (playlists, saved sounds) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not store your personal TikTok data on our servers beyond what is necessary for the app to function.

5. TikTok API

Soundly uses TikTok's official API to access your account data. Your use of TikTok's services is also governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy, available at https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You can delete your data at any time by disconnecting your TikTok account within the app or by uninstalling Soundly.

7. Children's Privacy

Soundly is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by disconnecting your TikTok account

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: Soundly.app@gmail.com
